Small Studio.
Big Impact.

Independent. UK-Born. Fearlessly Creative.

Founded in 2020, Kinetic Games is a truly independent UK studio dedicated to crafting distinctive, genre-defining experiences. With a small but mighty team, we focus on pushing boundaries and staying closely connected to the community that inspires us.

We create the games we want to play—worlds built with care, tension, and personality. If it thrills us, challenges us, or unnerves us, we build it and share it with everyone.
